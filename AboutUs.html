About INJA Foundation

Transforming lives in Pune district since 2019 through Education, Health, Agriculture, Environment, and Economic Development.

Who We Are

INJA Foundation was registered in 2019 under the Mumbai Public Trust Act 1950. Located at Plot.No.12/27, D1 Block, Chinchwad MIDC, Near Thermax Chowk, Pune-411019, we have been working for the past 5 years in urban and rural areas of Pune district. Our efforts focus on Education, Health, Agriculture, Environment, and Economic Development, with a special emphasis on transforming land parcels into biodiversity-rich urban forests to combat climate change and enhance community wellness.

Our Mission

  • To bring modern science and technology to explore the scientific treasures of Ayurveda by adopting scientific methods.
  • To achieve global leadership in research for the treatment and prevention of emerging lifestyle-related diseases and health needs.
  • To convince society that there is no alternative but tree plantation.

Our Vision

To bring about positive change in people, processes, and practices through coordinated efforts of all stakeholders, by providing training, education, and facilities to people in urban and rural areas.

Our Objectives

  • Provide free education for the underprivileged.
  • Establish educational training institutes with hostel facilities in backward areas.
  • Organize workshops and camps for the underprivileged.
  • Promote women and girl child education programs.
  • Support education for the handicapped.
  • Facilitate skill development.
  • Conduct awareness campaigns on anti-dowry, child marriage, AIDS, sex education, etc.
  • Work on water management and environmental protection projects.
