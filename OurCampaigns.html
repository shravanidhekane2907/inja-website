Our Programs

Empowering communities through Health, Agriculture, and Environmental initiatives in Pune district.

Health Awareness Camps

We organize health awareness programs, particularly for women in Chikhali, Pune, to promote community health and encourage responsibility for maintaining clean surroundings.

Through various awareness sessions, we engage community members, especially women, to approach civic authorities and advocate for a healthier environment.

Free Health Checkup Camps

For the past three years, we have conducted free general health and eye checkup camps for the underprivileged in Pune, addressing neglected healthcare needs.

We provide medical assistance, referral services, and display posters to raise awareness about various diseases, particularly in rural areas.

Annually, we organize two distinct free health checkup and eye checkup camps to improve community health and quality of life.

Tree Plantation

The primary objective of INJA Foundation is to plant trees in urban and rural areas to combat climate change and enhance biodiversity.

Our tree plantation initiatives transform land parcels into biodiversity-rich urban forests, adding beauty and promoting wellness for residents.
