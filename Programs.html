Our Programs

Empowering students through education at the Japanese Language Learning Center.

Japanese Language Learning Center

INJA Foundation inaugurated the Japanese Language Learning Center at Swami Vivekananda Vidyalaya, Post - Surudi, Taluka - Ashti, District - Beed.

This facility enables students from local villages to learn Japanese, enhancing their academic abilities and opening doors to study abroad programs and scholarships in Japan, enriching their lives with new opportunities.

The program was attended and inaugurated by Mr. Hyodo Ryota from Cosmo Group Japan.
